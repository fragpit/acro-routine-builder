# FAI Sporting Code Section 7B - Paragliding Aerobatics 2025 (Solo)

## 1. Manoeuvre definitions

### 1.1 Solo manoeuvres

#### 1.1.1 Tail Slide - coeff: 1.15

- Stabilised backward flying with open glider
- Minimum: 5 seconds
- Criteria: maintenance of the shape, stability, perceptible
  backwards flight, control of direction, duration,
  exit or connection
- Twisted Tail Slide: twisted all the way from entry to exit
- Free connection
- Repetition allowed (see 3.3.4)
- Bonuses: Twisted 6%, Twisted Exit 4.5%

#### 1.1.2 SAT - coeff: 1.25

- Evenly balanced SAT rotation
- Minimum: 2 rotations
- Criteria: entry, angle of wing (90 degrees for max score),
  low sink rate exit or connection (no collapse penalty
  for tip collapse during exit)
- Twisted SAT: twisted on the entry, must stay twisted for
  2 turns, can be exited untwisted
- Forbidden connection to combo manoeuvres
- Bonuses: Twisted 2.5%

#### 1.1.3 Asymetric Spiral - coeff: 1.35

- Series of pendulum turns in the same direction each time
- Criteria: rhythm, flow, trajectory, angle (135 degrees for
  maximum score - the higher the better)
- Twisted Asymetric Spiral: twisted all the way from entry
  to exit
- Free connection
- Bonuses: Twisted 2.5%

#### 1.1.4 Wingovers - coeff: 1.35

- Series of pendulum turns with change of direction each time
- Minimum: twice to one side, twice to the other side,
  135 degrees angle
- Criteria: rhythm, flow, trajectory, angle (minimum
  135 degrees for maximum score - the higher the better)
- Twisted Wingovers: must be twisted during the entry,
  and stays twisted until the glider stabilises
- Free connection
- Repetition allowed (see 3.3.4)
- Bonuses: Twisted 3.5%

#### 1.1.5 Looping (Inversion) - coeff: 1.50

- Entry from asymmetric spiral or a normal spiral. Reversal
  of a revolution that makes the pilot turn around the wing
  in a roll movement
- Minimum: 135 degrees angle
- Criteria: entry, energy, timing, flow, trajectory
  (pitch movement), angle (180 degrees for maximum score),
  exit or connection
- Twisted Looping: twisted on the entry, must stay twisted
  until the glider stabilises
- Free connection
- Bonuses: Twisted 3.5%

#### 1.1.6 Asymetric SAT - coeff: 1.55

- Entry from spiral or wingover in same direction (old school)
- Criteria: energy, timing, flow, trajectory (SAT rotation),
  angle (135 degrees for maximum score), asymmetric dynamic
  exit (no collapse, no stall)
- Twisted Asymetric SAT: twisted all the way from entry to exit
- Free connection
- Bonuses: Twisted 2.5%

#### 1.1.7 Stall - coeff: 1.60

- Straight climb + stall >90 degrees for max score
- Minimum: 45 degrees backwards pitch, over 90 degrees
  for max score
- Criteria: energy, importance of pitch, cleanness of the stall,
  control of direction and exit or connection
- Twisted Stall: twisted on the entry, stay twisted when the
  glider stalls, untwist during the dive
- Twisted Stall with Twisted Exit: the twist must be kept
  for at least 3 seconds or until the next manoeuvre
- Stall with Devil Twisted entry: twisted on the entry,
  untwist during the stall and immediately twist to the other
  direction, must exit twisted. The twist must be kept for
  at least 3 seconds or until the next manoeuvre
- Stall Flip: body flip either forward or backwards during
  the stall. Flip may begin during the climb out and must
  be completed before the dive
- Stall Double Flip: both flips must be chained, flow
- "Twisted" and "Devil Twist" bonuses are mutually exclusive
- Free connection
- Repetition allowed (see 3.3.4)
- Bonuses: Twisted 2.5%, Twisted Exit 4.5%, Devil Twist 7%,
  Flip 4.5%, Double Flip 5.5%

#### 1.1.8 Misty Flip - coeff: 1.65

- Straight entry & climb, 360 degrees spin, straight
  horizontal dive, exit not more than 180 degrees from
  the direction of entry
- Criteria: importance of pitch on entry, 360 degrees
  rotation, maintenance of shape, strong dive, no rotation
  on entry and exit, direction
- Twisted Misty Flip: twisted on the entry, untwisted by
  the rotation of the glider
- Twisted Misty Flip with Twisted Exit: the twist shall be
  kept for at least 3 seconds
- Misty Flip Full Twisted: keep the twist during the whole
  manoeuvre from beginning till exit as well as during
  the whole rotation. No change of direction of the twist.
  Twist shall be kept for at least 3 seconds or until
  the next trick
- Free connection
- Bonuses: Twisted 2.5%, Twisted Exit 3.5%, Full Twisted 6%

#### 1.1.9 X-Chopper - coeff: 1.70

- Entry with high energy, constant & rapid climb, spin,
  strong dive, exit
- Criteria: importance of pitch on entry, minimum 360 degrees
  rotation, energy, maintenance of shape, strong dive
- Twisted X-Chopper: twisted on the entry, untwisted by
  the rotation of the glider
- Free connection
- Bonuses: Twisted 3.5%, Twisted Exit 4.5%

#### 1.1.10 X-Chopper to SAT - coeff: 1.85

- X-Chopper directly connected into a SAT maneuver with
  a fast and clean transition
- Minimum: X-Chopper criteria, a more sideway exit of the
  X-Chopper is allowed, no spiral during the connection for
  max score, SAT criteria
- Criteria: speed, flow, connection, angle, fluidity
- Twisted X-Chopper to SAT: twisted on the X-Chopper entry,
  untwisted by the rotation
- Full Twisted: twisted on the X-Chopper entry, staying
  twisted during the whole rotation and connection, must stay
  twisted for min 1 turn in SAT. Can untwist for the SAT exit
- Devil Twist: twisted on the entry, untwisted by the rotation
  and twisted again during the end of the rotation, staying
  twisted during the connection, must stay twisted for min
  1 turn in SAT. Can untwist for the SAT exit
- Free connection
- Bonuses: Twisted 4%, Full Twisted 9%, Devil Twist 8.5%

#### 1.1.11 Misty to SAT - coeff: 1.70

- Misty + transition to SAT
- Minimum: 45 degrees climb before rotation
- Criteria: same criteria as for Misty Flip and SAT + flow
  and speed of transition
- Twisted Misty to SAT: must be twisted during the entry,
  untwisted by the rotation of the Misty Flip
- Misty to SAT Full Twisted: must stay twisted during the
  rotation of the Misty Flip, keep the twist during the exit
  and stay twisted during the SAT until the exit
- Misty to SAT with Devil Twisted entry: twisted during the
  entry, untwisted by the spinning of the glider and twisted
  to the opposite direction, then connected to a twisted SAT
- Misty to Twisted SAT: normal (not twisted) Misty Flip,
  twisted during the end of the rotation of the misty
  connected into a twisted SAT
- "Twisted", "Full Twisted", "to Twisted SAT" and
  "Devil Twist" bonuses are mutually exclusive
- Forbidden connection to SAT
- Bonuses: Twisted 3%, Full Twisted 6%, Devil Twist 6%,
  To Twisted SAT 3%

#### 1.1.12 Helicopter - coeff: 1.70

- Perfect spin with open glider and vertical axe of rotation
- Minimum: 3 rotations
- Criteria: maintenance of the shape, stability of rotation
  axis (vertical), speed of rotation, low sink rate, duration,
  exit or connection
- Twisted Helicopter: twisting during the helicopter, must
  stay twisted for 2 turns, can untwist for the exit
- Full Twisted Helicopter: twisted during the whole helicopter
  from entry to exit. Twisted during the entry, for minimum
  2 rotations and staying twisted on the exit
- Twisted exit: twisted for min 2 rotations before the exit.
  Staying twisted on the exit and for min 3 sec after the exit
  OR until the next trick
- Forbidden connection to any helicopter connection
- Bonuses: Twisted 3%, Full Twisted 9%, Twisted Exit 3%

#### 1.1.13 Mac Twist - coeff: 1.70

- Spin from spiral maintained until pendulum stabilised
- Criteria: energy and asymmetry on entry, speed and
  importance of rotation, pendulum stabilisation, fast exit
  or connection
- Twisted Mac Twist: must be twisted during the entry,
  untwisted by the rotation of the Mac Twist
- Free connection
- Bonuses: Twisted 3.5%

#### 1.1.14 Misty to Helicopter - coeff: 1.75

- Misty Flip + transition to Helicopter
- Criteria: flow and speed of transition
- Twisted Misty to Helicopter: must be twisted during
  the entry, untwisted by the rotation of the Misty
- Misty to Helicopter Reverse: immediate transition to
  a Helicopter in the opposite direction
- Forbidden connection to Helicopter
- Bonuses: Twisted 3%, Reverse 3%, Flipped 3%

#### 1.1.15 Misty to Misty - coeff: 1.75

- Misty chained with another misty on the other side using
  the exit energy of the first one
- Criteria: same criteria as of Misty Flip
- Twisted Misty to Misty: twisted on the entry, untwisted
  by the rotation of the glider
- Free connection

> **AWT:** Misty to Misty does not exist in the AWT and
> cannot be used as a manoeuvre. If a pilot flies 2 Misty
> Flips in a row it will be counted as 2 separate Misty
> Flips in the trick list.

- Bonuses: Twisted 3.5%, Twisted Exit 4%

#### 1.1.16 Twister (Helicopter to Helicopter) - coeff: 1.80

- Helicopter to one side, Helicopter to the other side
- Minimum: 3 rotations each side
- Criteria: speed of transition
- Forbidden connection to Helicopter

#### 1.1.17 Tumbling - coeff: 1.80

- Asymmetric or inversion entry, perfect pitch movement
- Minimum: 1 rotation, 135 degrees angle
- Criteria: energy on entry, timing, flow, trajectory
  (pitch movement), angle (180 degrees for maximum score),
  exit or connection
- Twisted Tumbling: twisted on the entry, must stay twisted,
  untwist at the exit NOT during the turns
- Hardcore entry: twisted on the speed taking, getting
  untwisted and twisted in the other direction while pulling
  the entry
- Cab Slide: get twisted during the turns
- Devil Twist: from a twisted situation, change of twist
  direction (within one turn)
- "Twisted", "Hardcore", "Cab Slide" bonuses are
  mutually exclusive
- Forbidden connection to Infinite and Anti-Rhythmic
- This manoeuvre cannot be one of the last two manoeuvres
- Bonuses: Twisted 3.5%, Twisted Exit 4.5%, Devil Twist 2%,
  Flip 5%, Hardcore Entry 5%, Cab Slide 2%

#### 1.1.18 Anti-Rhythmic SAT - coeff: 1.80

- Entry by Tumbling, 180 degrees in the first rotation for
  maximum points + 1 SAT rotation at the end
- Minimum: 135 degrees angle for the first rotation
- Criteria: rhythm and regularity of the decreasing angle
  of the axis, flow, exit or connection
- Twisted Anti-Rhythmic SAT from Cab Slide: twist during the
  Infinite turns, must stay twisted until the SAT. Must stay
  twisted for 1 SAT rotation, can untwist for the exit
- Hardcore entry: twisted on the speed taking, getting
  untwisted and twisted in the other direction while pulling
  the entry
- Twisted Anti-Rhythmic SAT from Tumbling: twisted on the
  entry, must stay twisted until the SAT. Must stay twisted
  for 1 SAT turn, can untwist for the exit
- Free connection
- This manoeuvre cannot be one of the last two manoeuvres
- Bonuses: Twisted 3%, Hardcore Entry 4%

#### 1.1.19 SAT to Helicopter - coeff: 1.85

- Several SAT rotations + connection to Helicopter
- Minimum: 2 SAT rotations, 3 Helicopter rotations
- Criteria: flow and speed of transition
- Twisted SAT to Helicopter: twisted on the entry, must be
  untwisted during the transition into helicopter, NOT before
- SAT to Helicopter Reverse: immediate transition to
  a Helicopter in the opposite direction
- Forbidden connection to Helicopter
- Bonuses: Twisted 4%, Reverse 4%

#### 1.1.20 Helicopter to SAT - coeff: 1.85

- Helicopter + connection to SAT
- Minimum: 3 Helicopter rotations, 2 SAT rotations
- Criteria: no rotation on entry and exit, direction
- Twisted Helicopter to SAT: twist during the helicopter.
  Must stay twisted during the whole transition, at least
  1 turn of SAT twisted. Can untwist on the exit
- Helicopter to SAT Reverse: immediate transition to a SAT
  in the opposite direction
- Free connection
- Bonuses: Twisted 4.5%, Reverse 4%

#### 1.1.21 Mactwist to SAT - coeff: 1.85

- A Mactwist directly connected into a SAT maneuver with
  a fast and clean transition
- Minimum: Mactwist criteria, a more sideway exit of the
  Mactwist is allowed, no spiral during the connection for
  max score, SAT criteria
- Criteria: speed, flow, connection, angle, fluidity
- Twisted Mactwist to SAT: twisted on the Mactwist entry,
  untwisted by the rotation
- Full Twisted Mactwist to SAT: twisted on the Mactwist
  entry, staying twisted during the whole rotation and
  connection, must stay twisted for min 1 turn in SAT.
  Can untwist for the SAT exit
- Devil Twist Mactwist to SAT: twisted on the Mactwist entry,
  untwisted by the rotation and twisted again during the end
  of the rotation, staying twisted during the connection,
  must stay twisted for min 1 turn in SAT. Can untwist
  for the SAT exit
- Free connection
- Bonuses: Twisted 4%, Full Twisted 8%, Devil Twist 6.5%

#### 1.1.22 Mac Twist to Helicopter - coeff: 1.85

- Mac Twist with the spiral maintained to Helicopter
- Minimum: same as Mac Twist and Helicopter
- Criteria: flow and speed of transition
- Twisted Mac Twist to Helicopter: must be twisted during
  the entry, untwisted by the rotation of the Mac Twist
- Mac Twist to Helicopter Reverse: immediate transition to
  a Helicopter in the opposite direction
- Devil Twist Mactwist to Heli: twisted during the entry,
  untwisted during the beginning of the connection, re twisted
  by the connection to heli. Must stay twisted for minimum
  2 turns of heli. Can untwist for the exit
- Free connection
- Bonuses: Twisted 4%, Reverse 3.5%, Devil Twist 7%

#### 1.1.23 Infinity Tumbling - coeff: 1.85

- Series of perfect Tumbling (pitch movement)
- Minimum: entry plus 5 rotations
- Criteria: rhythm, flow, trajectory (no roll movement),
  tension in the lines and glider, duration, exit or
  connection
- Twisted Infinity Tumbling: twisted on the entry, must stay
  twisted, untwist at exit and NOT during the turns
- Infinity Tumbling Flip: body flip either forward or
  backwards during the Infinite Tumbling. Flip must begin
  and end at the same point of one full rotation
- Hardcore entry: twisted on the speed taking, getting
  untwisted and twisted in the other direction while pulling
  the entry
- Cab Slide: get twisted during the turns
- Devil Twist: from a twisted situation, change of twist
  direction (within one turn)
- "Twisted", "Hardcore" and "Cab Slide" bonuses are
  mutually exclusive
- Forbidden connection to Tumbling or Anti-Rhythmic SAT
- This manoeuvre cannot be one of the last two manoeuvres
- Bonuses: Twisted 3.5%, Twisted Exit 4.5%, Devil Twist 2%,
  Flip 5%, Hardcore Entry 5%, Cab Slide 2%

#### 1.1.24 Cowboy - coeff: 1.90

- Misty Flip to dive to Helicopter
- Minimum: 45 degrees climb before rotation
- Criteria: Misty Flip criteria + forward dive + short SAT
  move + transition to Helicopter + Helicopter criteria
- Twisted Cowboy: must be twisted during the entry, untwisted
  by the rotation of the Misty
- Cowboy Reverse: immediate transition to a Helicopter in
  the opposite direction
- Devil Twist Cowboy: twisted during the entry, untwisted by
  the rotation of the misty and re twisted before the dive,
  then untwisted and re twisted again during the connection
  into heli, must stay twisted during at least 2 rotation
  of helicopter. Can untwist for the exit
- Forbidden connection to Helicopter
- Bonuses: Twisted 4%, Reverse 4.5%, Devil Twist 8%

#### 1.1.25 SuperCowboy - coeff: 1.90

- Mactwist to dive to Helicopter
- Minimum: 45 degrees climb before rotation
- Criteria: Mactwist criteria + forward dive + short SAT
  move + transition to Helicopter + Helicopter criteria
- Twisted SuperCowboy: twisted during the entry, untwisted
  by the rotation of the Mactwist
- Devil Twist SuperCowboy: twisted during the entry,
  untwisted by the rotation of the Mactwist and re twisted
  before the dive, then untwisted and re twisted again during
  the connection into heli, must stay twisted during at least
  2 rotations of helicopter. Can untwist for the exit
- SuperCowboy Reverse: immediate transition to a Helicopter
  in the opposite direction
- Forbidden connection to Helicopter
- Bonuses: Twisted 4%, Reverse 4.5%, Devil Twist 8%

#### 1.1.26 Corkscrew - coeff: 1.90

- Spin during the entry over the glider into a Helicopter
- Minimum: 120 degrees
- Criteria: energy on entry, timing, flow. Stable entry into
  the Helicopter
- Twisted Corkscrew: twisted during the entry, untwisted by
  the rotation of the cork
- Corkscrew Reverse: immediate transition to a Helicopter in
  the opposite direction
- Corkscrew Devil Twist: twisted during the entry, untwisted
  during the rotation of the cork, re twisted by the
  connection. Must stay twisted for minimum 2 turns of heli.
  Can untwist for the exit
- Forbidden connection to Helicopter
- Bonuses: Twisted 4.5%, Reverse 4.5%, Devil Twist 7%

#### 1.1.27 Joker - coeff: 1.90

- Tumbling entry. 1 rotation only, stopped during the dive.
  Exit with high energy connected to a Helicopter to the same
  side + SAT to Helicopter connection
- Criteria: energy on entry, timing, flow, energy on exit,
  angle, connection to Helicopter (fluidity, rapidity).
  Only 1 Tumbling rotation
- Twisted Joker: twisted on the entry, twisted on the dive,
  must untwist during the connection into helicopter
- Devil Twist Joker: twisted during the entry, untwisted
  during the beginning of the connection, re twisted by the
  connection to heli. Must stay twisted for minimum 2 turns
  of heli. Can untwist for the exit
- Joker Reverse: immediate transition to a Helicopter in
  the opposite direction
- Forbidden connection to Helicopter
- Bonuses: Twisted 4.5%, Reverse 4.5%, Devil Twist 7%

#### 1.1.28 Mac Twist to Tumbling - coeff: 1.95

- 1 turn (360 degrees) Mac Twist rotation (half a Mac Twist)
  followed by a high Tumbling
- Criteria: energy on entry, timing, flow
- Twisted Mac Twist to Tumbling: twisted on the entry,
  untwisted by the rotation of the glider
- Cab Slide: get twisted during the turns
- Devil Twist: from a twisted situation, change of twist
  direction (within one turn)
- Forbidden connection to Infinite Tumbling nor Anti-Rhythmic
- This manoeuvre cannot be one of the last two manoeuvres
- Bonuses: Twisted 2.5%, Cab Slide 2%, Devil Twist 2%,
  Twisted Exit 4.5%

#### 1.1.29 Rhythmic SAT - coeff: 1.95

- Entry without pitch, constant progression until reaching
  180 degrees + 1 straight rotation at the end
- Minimum: 135 degrees in the best rotation
- Criteria: rhythm and regularity of the variation of
  the axis, flow, angle, exit or connection
- Twisted Rhythmic SAT: twisted on the entry, must stay
  twisted, untwist at the exit and NOT during the turns
- Devil Twist: from a twisted situation, change of twist
  direction (within one turn)
- Forbidden connection to Tumbling, Infinite and
  Anti-Rhythmic
- This manoeuvre cannot be one of the last two manoeuvres
- Bonuses: Twisted 4%, Twisted Exit 4.5%, Devil Twist 2%

#### 1.1.30 Esfera - coeff: 1.95

- Rhythmic SAT pulled to the vertical and followed by
  an Anti-Rhythmic SAT to the other side
- Criteria: same as for Rhythmic SAT and Anti-Rhythmic SAT
- Twisted Esfera: twisted on the entry. Must stay twisted
  until going vertical, untwist when vertical and twist again
  on the other side for the Anti-Rhythmic. Must stay twisted
  for at least one SAT rotation. Can exit untwisted. The pilot
  must twist immediately after untwisting. Untwist during
  1 rotation and twist again during the next rotation
- Free connection
- This manoeuvre cannot be one of the last two manoeuvres
- Bonuses: Twisted 6%

#### 1.1.31 X-Chopper to Tumbling - coeff: 1.95

- X-Chopper (min 360 degrees spin rotation) followed by
  a high Tumbling using the dive from the X-Chopper.
  Minimum 3 rotations of tumbling
- Criteria: energy on entry, timing, flow, tumbling minimum
  3 rotations. Perfectly straight axis on the first rotation
  of tumbling for max score
- Twisted: twisted on the entry, untwisted by the rotation
  of the glider
- Full Twisted: twisted during the whole X-Chopper, staying
  twisted during the shoot and until the exit of the tumbling
- Hardcore Entry: untwisted during the rotation of the
  X-Chopper immediately followed by a twist to the other
  direction, staying twisted during the shoot and until
  the exit of the tumbling
- Cab Slide: get twisted during the turns
- Devil Twist: from a twisted situation, change of twist
  direction (within one turn)
- "Twisted", "Full Twisted" and "Devil Twist Stall" bonuses
  are mutually exclusive
- "Full Twisted", "Devil Twist Stall" and "Cab Slide"
  bonuses are mutually exclusive
- This manoeuvre cannot be one of the last two manoeuvres
- Cannot be performed in the same run as a stall to infinite
  type of manoeuvre (see 3.2.2)
- Forbidden connection to Infinite or Anti-Rhythmic
- Bonuses: Twisted 3.5%, Full Twisted 7.5%,
  Hardcore Entry 7.5%, Cab Slide 2%, Devil Twist 2%,
  Twisted Exit 3%

#### 1.1.32 Super Stall to Infinity Tumbling - coeff: 2.00

- Super Stall directly connected into an Infinite Tumbling.
  The Full Stall must not necessarily be high but shall be
  perfectly straight
- Minimum: entry + 3 rotations
- Criteria: rhythm, flow, trajectory (no roll movement),
  tension in the lines and glider, duration, exit or
  connection
- Twisted: twisted on the entry, untwist after the stall and
  proceed into the Infinity Tumbling without twist
- Twisted with Twisted Exit: stay twisted during the exit of
  the Infinity Tumbling
- Full Twisted: twisted on the entry, stay twisted after the
  stall and proceed into the Infinity Tumbling twisted and
  untwist at exit (not during the turns)
- Devil Twisted Stall: twisted on the entry, untwist during
  the stall and immediately twist to the other direction and
  proceed into the Infinity Tumbling twisted
- Flip: the pilot performs a flip just after the glider
  stalled, proceed into the Infinity Tumbling
- Cab Slide: get twisted during the turns
- Devil Twist: from a twisted situation, change of twist
  direction (within one turn)
- "Twisted", "Full Twisted" and "Devil Twist Stall" bonuses
  are mutually exclusive
- "Full Twisted", "Devil Twist Stall" and "Cab Slide"
  bonuses are mutually exclusive
- Forbidden connection to Tumbling or Anti-Rhythmic SAT
- This manoeuvre cannot be one of the last two manoeuvres
- Bonuses: Twisted 2.5%, Twisted Exit 3%, Full Twisted 6.5%,
  Devil Twist Stall 7.5%, Flip 6.5%, Cab Slide 2%,
  Devil Twist 2%

#### 1.1.33 Flat Stall to Infinity Tumbling - coeff: 2.05

- Full Stall directly connected into an Infinite Tumbling.
  The Full Stall must not necessarily be high but shall be
  perfectly straight. The pilot must not take any energy
  before the stall. It must come from a straight flying
  scenario, no pitch before the stall
- Minimum: entry + 3 rotations
- Criteria: rhythm, flow, trajectory (no roll movement),
  tension in the lines and glider, duration, exit or
  connection
- Twisted: twisted on the entry, untwist after the stall and
  proceed into the Infinity Tumbling without twist
- Twisted with Twisted Exit: stay twisted during the exit of
  the Infinity Tumbling
- Full Twisted: twisted on the entry, stay twisted after the
  stall and proceed into the Infinity Tumbling twisted and
  untwist at exit (not during the turns)
- Devil Twisted Stall: twisted on the entry, untwist during
  the stall and immediately twist to the other direction and
  proceed into the Infinity Tumbling twisted
- Flip: the pilot performs a flip just after the glider
  stalled, proceed into the Infinity Tumbling
- Cab Slide: get twisted during the turns
- Devil Twist: from a twisted situation, change of twist
  direction (within one turn)
- "Twisted", "Full Twisted" and "Devil Twist Stall" bonuses
  are mutually exclusive
- "Full Twisted", "Devil Twist Stall" and "Cab Slide"
  bonuses are mutually exclusive
- Forbidden connection to Tumbling or Anti-Rhythmic SAT
- This manoeuvre cannot be one of the last two manoeuvres
- **This manoeuvre MUST be the first manoeuvre**
- Bonuses: Twisted 2.5%, Twisted Exit 3%, Full Twisted 6.5%,
  Devil Twist Stall 7.5%, Flip 6.5%, Cab Slide 2%,
  Devil Twist 2%

#### 1.1.34 MacFly (Mactwist to Tailslide to Infinity Tumbling) - coeff: 2.10

- Mactwist to Tail Slide or Deep Stall directly connected
  into an Infinite Tumbling. The Tail Slide/Deep Stall must
  not necessarily be long but shall be entered directly from
  the exit of a Mactwist
- Minimum: entry + 3 rotations
- Criteria: rhythm, flow, trajectory (no roll movement),
  tension in the lines and glider, duration, exit or
  connection
- Twisted: twisted on the entry, untwist after the Tail
  Slide/Deep Stall and proceed into the Infinity Tumbling
  without twist
- Twisted with Twisted Exit: stay twisted during the exit of
  the Infinity Tumbling
- Full Twisted: twisted on the entry, stay twisted after the
  Tail Slide/Deep Stall and proceed into the Infinity Tumbling
  twisted and untwist at exit (not during the turns)
- Hardcore Entry: twisted on the entry, untwisted by the
  rotation of the Mactwist and twisted again on the end of
  the Mactwist's rotation, stay twisted after the Tail
  Slide/Deep Stall and proceed into the Infinity Tumbling
  twisted and untwist at exit (not during the turns)
- Cab Slide: get twisted during the turns
- Devil Twist: from a twisted situation, change of twist
  direction (within one turn)
- "Twisted", "Full Twisted", "Cab Slide" and "Hardcore Entry"
  bonuses are mutually exclusive
- Forbidden connection to Tumbling or Anti-Rhythmic SAT
- This manoeuvre cannot be one of the last two manoeuvres
- This manoeuvre doesn't have a side and can be performed
  only once
- Bonuses: Twisted 3.5%, Twisted Exit 3%, Full Twisted 8%,
  Hardcore Entry 8%, Cab Slide 2%, Devil Twist 2%

#### 1.1.35 MistyFly (Misty to Tailslide to Infinity Tumbling) - coeff: 2.10

- Misty to Tail Slide or Deep Stall directly connected
  into an Infinite Tumbling. The Tail Slide/Deep Stall must

  the exit of a Misty
- Minimum: entry + 3 rotations
- Same twist variants as MacFly
- "Twisted", "Full Twisted", "Cab Slide" and "Hardcore Entry"
  bonuses are mutually exclusive
- Forbidden connection to Tumbling or Anti-Rhythmic SAT
- This manoeuvre cannot be one of the last two manoeuvres
- This manoeuvre doesn't have a side and can be performed
  only once
- Bonuses: Twisted 2.5%, Twisted Exit 3%, Full Twisted 8%,
  Hardcore Entry 8%, Cab Slide 2%, Devil Twist 2%

#### 1.1.36 HeliFly (Helico to Tailslide to Infinity Tumbling) - coeff: 2.10

- Helico to Tail Slide or Deep Stall directly connected into
  an Infinite Tumbling. The Tail Slide/Deep Stall must not
  necessarily be long but shall be entered directly from
  the exit of a Helico
- Minimum: entry + 3 rotations
- Same twist variants as MacFly (Hardcore Entry: twisted on
  the entry, untwisted when stopping the Helico and twisted
  again immediately to the other side)
- "Twisted", "Full Twisted", "Cab Slide" and "Hardcore Entry"
  bonuses are mutually exclusive
- Forbidden connection to Tumbling or Anti-Rhythmic SAT
- This manoeuvre cannot be one of the last two manoeuvres
- This manoeuvre doesn't have a side and can be performed
  only once
- Bonuses: Twisted 3%, Twisted Exit 3%, Full Twisted 8%,
  Hardcore Entry 8%, Cab Slide 2%, Devil Twist 2%

#### 1.1.37 SatFly (SAT to Tailslide to Infinity Tumbling) - coeff: 2.10

- SAT to Tail Slide or Deep Stall directly connected into
  an Infinite Tumbling. The SAT must be stalled "like" a SAT
  to Helico but instead of the Helico connected into a tail
  slide directly
- Minimum: entry + 3 rotations
- Same twist variants as MacFly (Hardcore Entry: twisted on
  the entry, untwisted when stopping the SAT and twisted
  again immediately to the other side)
- "Twisted", "Full Twisted", "Cab Slide" and "Hardcore Entry"
  bonuses are mutually exclusive
- Forbidden connection to Tumbling or Anti-Rhythmic SAT
- This manoeuvre cannot be one of the last two manoeuvres
- This manoeuvre doesn't have a side and can be performed
  only once
- Bonuses: Twisted 3.5%, Twisted Exit 3%, Full Twisted 8%,
  Hardcore Entry 8%, Cab Slide 2%, Devil Twist 2%

#### 1.1.38 Misty to Tumbling - coeff: 2.15

- Misty Flip (360 degrees spin rotation) followed by a high
  Tumbling using the dive from the Misty Flip
- Criteria: energy on entry, timing, flow
- Twisted Misty to Tumbling: twisted on the entry, untwisted
  by the rotation of the glider
- Cab Slide: get twisted during the turns
- Devil Twist: from a twisted situation, change of twist
  direction (within one turn)
- This manoeuvre cannot be one of the last two manoeuvres
- Cannot be performed in the same run as a stall to infinite
  type of manoeuvre (see 3.2.2)
- Bonuses: Twisted 6%, Cab Slide 2%, Devil Twist 2%,
  Twisted Exit 4.5%

### 1.2 Landing manoeuvres

#### 1.2.1 Solo landing

- **Approach and precision** - land on the raft for maximum
  score. Quality of the approach and precision
  (distance to the raft)
- **Raft** - pilot landing smoothly on his feet for maximum
  score. Landing fully on the raft
- **Ground Spiral** - minimum 360 degrees revolution, height
  of wing tip over water less than 3 metres at the lowest
  point. Criteria: entry (speed, sink rate), height of wing
  tip over water (touch for max score)
- **Hand touch** - precision, length of touch with hand
- **Foot touch** - precision, length of touch with foot
- **Jesus walk** - precision, length of walk and frequency
  of the steps
- **Spin** - minimum rotation: 1/2 turn before pilot lands.
  Speed of rotation, good sink rate and standing landing
- **Raft flip** - control and effectiveness

## 2. Sequence and order rules

### 2.1 Programs

Programs may be free, imposed or restricted. In an imposed or
restricted program, the Meet Director and/or the Chief Judge
are entitled to specify technical limitations.

### 2.2 High coefficient manoeuvres limit

During each run, a maximum of 2 manoeuvres with coefficient
of 1.95 or more can be flown. If more than 2 such manoeuvres
are flown, the extra manoeuvres will not be scored and their
coefficients not taken into account for the determination
of the average coefficient.

### 2.3 Forbidden connections

Each manoeuvre has specified forbidden connections:

| Manoeuvre                         | Forbidden connection to           |
| --------------------------------- | --------------------------------- |
| SAT                               | combo manoeuvres                  |
| Misty to SAT                      | SAT                               |
| Helicopter                        | any helicopter connection         |
| Misty to Helicopter               | Helicopter                        |
| Twister                           | Helicopter                        |
| Tumbling                          | Infinite and Anti-Rhythmic        |
| SAT to Helicopter                 | Helicopter                        |
| Mac Twist to Helicopter           | (none specified beyond general)   |
| Cowboy                            | Helicopter                        |
| SuperCowboy                       | Helicopter                        |
| Corkscrew                         | Helicopter                        |
| Joker                             | Helicopter                        |
| Mac Twist to Tumbling             | Infinite Tumbling, Anti-Rhythmic  |
| Rhythmic SAT                      | Tumbling, Infinite, Anti-Rhythmic |
| Infinity Tumbling                 | Tumbling, Anti-Rhythmic SAT       |
| X-Chopper to Tumbling             | Infinite, Anti-Rhythmic           |
| Super Stall to Infinity           | Tumbling, Anti-Rhythmic SAT       |
| Flat Stall to Infinity            | Tumbling, Anti-Rhythmic SAT       |
| MacFly, MistyFly, HeliFly, SatFly | Tumbling, Anti-Rhythmic SAT       |

### 2.4 "Last two manoeuvres" restrictions

The following manoeuvres cannot be one of the last two
manoeuvres in a run:

- Tumbling
- Anti-Rhythmic SAT
- Infinity Tumbling
- Mac Twist to Tumbling
- Rhythmic SAT
- Esfera
- X-Chopper to Tumbling
- Super Stall to Infinity Tumbling
- Flat Stall to Infinity Tumbling
- MacFly, MistyFly, HeliFly, SatFly
- Misty to Tumbling

A flipped maneuver cannot be one of the last 2 maneuvers.

### 2.5 "Must be first" restriction

- Flat Stall to Infinity Tumbling MUST be the first
  manoeuvre in a run

### 2.6 One-per-run restrictions

Only one of the following manoeuvres can be performed during
the same run:

- Super Stall to Infinite Tumbling
- Flat Stall to Infinite Tumbling
- Tail Slide to Infinite Tumbling
- MacFly (Mactwist to Tailslide to Infinity Tumbling)
- MistyFly (Misty to Tailslide to Infinity Tumbling)
- HeliFly (Helico to Tailslide to Infinity Tumbling)
- SatFly (SAT to Tailslide to Infinity Tumbling)

Only 2 tumbling, infinity and rhythmic related manoeuvres
can be performed during the same run.

X-Chopper to Tumbling and Misty to Tumbling cannot be
performed in the same run as a stall to infinite type
of manoeuvre.

MacFly, MistyFly, HeliFly, SatFly - each doesn't have a side
and can be performed only once.

### 2.7 Announcement of program start

Before starting the program, every pilot/pair must make one
"big ear" to notify the judges the start of the run. If
a pilot/pair forgets to comply, a pre-warning will be issued,
meaning that the next omission will lead to an official
warning.

If before starting the first manoeuvre the conditions are
estimated unsafe or the altitude too low, the judges are
notified by making 2 "big ears". Then the pilot has to fly
down immediately to a safe landing without making any
aerobatic manoeuvre.

### 2.8 Bonus limits per run

- Maximum 5 manoeuvres can be performed twisted per run
- Maximum 3 manoeuvres can be performed reversed per run
- Maximum 2 manoeuvres can be performed flipped per run

If limits are exceeded, the extra manoeuvres will not be
scored and their coefficients not taken into account for the
determination of the average coefficient.

Flipped manoeuvres must be announced before the run by
personally informing one of the judges, or, at the latest
before take-off, by requesting the start marshal or drop
master to inform the judges.

> **Competitions above ground:** flipped tricks are forbidden.

### 2.9 Number of runs

> **AWT/AWQ:** the number of runs is limited to 3 maximum
> for all AWT/AWQ events (except the superfinal). For the
> superfinal: maximum of 5 runs.

A maximum of 7 rounds may be flown in each category in
regular competitions.

## 3. Scoring, penalties and scoring rules

### 3.1 Competition validity

A minimum of 2 runs is required to validate the championships.

> **Competitions above ground:** only 1 run is required to
> validate the competition.

### 3.2 Scoring in Solo

Each judge awards marks for:

- **Technical expression (T)** - rough average of all the
  technical marks awarded for each tricks flown during
  the run
- **General choreography (C)**
- **Landing (L)**

Marks are from 0 to 10, with 0.1 points. For each mark,
the final mark is the average of each judge's mark.

Distribution: 40% technical, 40% choreography, 20% landing.
These values can be modified depending on local factors.

#### Final score formula (Solo)

```text
Technical final score = T * TC * 40%
Choreo final score    = C * 40%
Landing final score   = L * 20%
Bonus = (Technical final score + Choreo final score) * B%
Final score = Technical final + Choreo final + Landing final + Bonus
```

#### Technicity (TC)

The technicity is a difficulty coefficient calculated as the
average of the 3 highest coefficient manoeuvres flown during
the run (max 2 above 1.95).

#### Choreography criteria (Solo)

Objective criteria (8/10) and subjective (2/10):

- **Trick directions balance** (1/8) - same number of tricks
  in both directions. Odd number: difference of 1 is OK
- **Chaining** (1.5/8) - manoeuvres must be as chained as
  possible, least amount of time wasted between tricks
- **No replacement during the run** (1/8) - pilot who did not
  fly straight to replace himself gets maximum points
- **Altitude management** (2/8):
  - Pilot should be able to attempt a raft landing (1/1)
  - No pause between last manoeuvre and landing, flow must
    continue until end (1/1)
- **Diversity** (1/8) - maximum diversity of manoeuvres
- **Originality** (1.5/8) - original sequence of tricks,
  rarely performed manoeuvres, rhythm contrast

The subjective mark (2/10) represents the general feeling of
the judge from the start of the run until the end of the
landing.

#### Landing scoring (Solo)

- Approach and precision of the raft landing (3/10)
- Ground Spiral (5/10)
- Other tricks: hand and foot touch, Jesus walk, spin,
  raft flip (2/10)

**Dry landing options** (including competitions above ground):

- Accuracy landing: target 3mx3m min or 3m radius circle.
  Landing in target up to 1 point + style tricks up to
  1 point = max 2 points
- Water slide landing: pool min 20cm water, 10m length,
  1m width. Touch water + land = up to 1 point + style up
  to 1 point = max 2 points
- Neither available: landing score is 0 for all pilots.
  No landing manoeuvres allowed or warning issued

Ground spiral landing is strictly forbidden when dry landing
(disqualification).

### 3.3 Repetition rules

#### 3.3.1 Repetition during the same competition

Each manoeuvre may only be performed once in each direction
during the same competition without deduction. A twisted or
flipped maneuver is considered the same maneuver as an
untwisted one. A reverse maneuver is considered a different
maneuver altogether.

Example: you can do a Joker to the left, followed by a
twisted Joker to the right, followed by a twisted Joker
reverse to the left without penalty. But if you do a twisted
Joker to the left and a Joker to the left you will get
one malus.

> **AWT:** in the AWT, Misty to Misty does not exist and
> cannot be used as a manoeuvre. If a pilot flies 2 Misty
> Flips in a row it will be counted as 2 separate Misty
> Flips.

#### 3.3.2 Repetition during the same run

Only one of these manoeuvres per run (see 2.6).

Only 2 tumbling, infinity and rhythmic related manoeuvres
can be performed during the same run.

#### 3.3.3 Deductions for repetition

The penalty for repetition is typically a reduction of 13%
of the choreography mark for each repetition, but may be
specified differently (especially for events where the
completion of more than 4 runs is expected).

#### 3.3.4 Exceptions to repetition

The following manoeuvres can be performed more than once
without penalty within the same competition:

- Tail Slide
- Wingover
- Full Stall
- Super Stall
- Pitch Pendulum
- Bro Hook

However, each manoeuvre can be performed only once twisted or
flipped within the same run. If repeated twisted or flipped
within the same run, the extra bonus will not be rewarded.

### 3.4 Bonus

Each manoeuvre can be awarded different kinds of bonuses:
twisted, reverse or flipped.

#### 3.4.1 Bonus calculation (AWT and CAT1)

> **AWT and CAT1:** the bonus is linked to the technical marks
> awarded for each trick. The total bonus for a trick is
> multiplied by the technical score of that trick divided
> by ten. At the end of the run all these new bonus scores
> are added together.
>
> Example:
>
> - Trick A: twisted bonus 5%, reverse bonus 5%.
>   Technical score 5. Bonus = (5+5)*5/10 = 5%
> - Trick B: twisted bonus 10%, flip bonus 2%.
>   Technical score 8. Bonus = (10+2)*8/10 = 9.6%
> - Trick C: reverse bonus 5%. Technical score 2.
>   Bonus = (5)*2/10 = 1%
> - Final bonus: 5 + 9.6 + 1 = 15.6%
>
> **AWQ:** this rule does NOT apply in AWQ. The bonus mark
> does not depend on the technical marks of each trick.

#### 3.4.2 Twisted manoeuvres bonus

Up to 5 manoeuvres can be performed twisted per run. They
are rewarded with "twisted bonus" points per the manoeuvre
listings.

Some manoeuvres can be rewarded with additional twist types
("twisted exit", "devil twist", "full twisted") and their
bonuses are added to each other if not specified otherwise.

The twisted exit bonus is awarded when a twisted manoeuvre
is exited twisted and/or is followed by another twisted
manoeuvre without untwisting in between. The pilot should stay
twisted until the following maneuver or stay twisted for
minimum 3 seconds after the exit.

#### 3.4.3 Reversed manoeuvres bonus

Up to 3 manoeuvres can be performed reversed per run.

#### 3.4.4 Flipped manoeuvres bonus

Up to 2 manoeuvres can be performed flipped per run.

Must be announced before the run. If a maneuver is performed
flipped while it doesn't exist as flipped in the scoring,
the bonus won't be awarded.

> **Competitions above ground:** flipped manoeuvres
> are forbidden.

### 3.5 Point deductions (penalties during manoeuvres)

#### 3.5.1 Collapses/Tucks

- 0 to 25%: 0 to 2.5 points reduction
- 25 to 50%: 2.5 to 5 points reduction
- 50 to 100%: 5 to 8 points reduction

#### 3.5.2 Unplanned change of direction

- <90 degrees: 0 to 2.5 points reduction
- 90 to 180 degrees: 2.5 to 5 points reduction
- >180 degrees: 5 to 8 points reduction

#### 3.5.3 Cravat

- <10% and recovery in less than 3s: 2.5 points reduction
- >50% and recovery in more than 3s: 2.5 to 8 points
  reduction

#### 3.5.4 Unplanned twist

- <1 turn: 2.5 to 5 points reduction
- 1 turn or more: 5 to 8 points reduction

### 3.6 Penalties and warnings

#### 3.6.1 Dangerous flying

When judges determine a pilot put himself into a dangerous
situation through recklessness, poor preparation or by
attempting a manoeuvre beyond his current skill level:
**0 points for the run**.

#### 3.6.2 Opening of reserve

Technical score only for the manoeuvres already completed.
0 for choreography and landing. A warning will be issued
in case of dangerous flying.

#### 3.6.3 Not opening reserve when needed

0 points for the run + Warning.

#### 3.6.4 Warning offences

**General Safety:**

- Disregard of the flight box
- Disregard of the signal closing the flight box
- Performing manoeuvres not allowed as one of the last
  2 manoeuvres
- Over flying the public
- Unauthorised take off

**Taking Risks:**

- Dangerous flying
- Endangering others (raft crew)
- Unsafe landing

**Respect / Sportsmanship:**

- Late arrival/non-attendance at briefings
- Delay at take-off
- Unsporting behaviour
- Disrespectful or aggressive behaviour
- No "ear" before programme start
- Any other serious misconduct

#### 3.6.5 Warning penalties

**Category 1 warnings** (missing briefing, missing shuttles,
not being ready in time, etc.):

- 1st warning: 0.5 point
- 2nd warning: 1 point
- 3rd warning: Disqualification

**Category 2 warnings** (dangerous flying, failure to use
the rescue, unsportsmanship behavior):

- 1st warning: 1 point
- 2nd warning: 2 points
- 3rd warning: Disqualification

Penalties apply whether the same offence is repeated or
different offences.

#### 3.6.6 Pilot gear management penalty

A pilot should be ready maximum 30 min after the daily
briefing. Not being ready and delaying the competition
schedule: schedule penalty of 0.5 points.

### 3.7 Champions and tied scores

#### 3.7.1 Solo

Overall score = sum of all task scores. Highest score wins.

#### 3.7.2 Team

Run score of a nation = sum of highest Y scores of that
nation's team pilots. Overall team score = sum of run scores.

#### 3.7.3 Tied scores

If tie for first three places, the pilot or pair with the
highest run score is declared higher placed. If same highest
score, second-highest scores are considered.

> **AWT/AWQ season:** if tie at the end of the season, the
> pilot with the highest number of competition wins is crowned
> champion. If none of the pilots won an event, the pilot with
> the highest number of task wins is crowned champion. For any
> ranking other than Champion, a tie is acceptable.

### 3.8 Score validation

After completing the run, pilots/pairs must validate their
manoeuvres by checking in at the judges' tent.

After a run is completed, the list of manoeuvres and
provisional results are published. Pilots have 1 hour to check
scores and report possible errors.

Results must include:

- Average judge's marks by type (technique, choreography,
  landing) between 0 and 10
- Bonuses percentage
- Technicity: average of the 3 highest technical coefficients
  (max 2 above 1.95)
- Overall score

## 4. AWT and AWQ specific rules

### 4.1 Structure

- **AWT (Acro World Tour):** 10 pilots qualified through AWT
  ranking from the previous season + 2 pilots qualified from
  AWQ results
- **AWQ (Acro World Qualifier):** the rest of the pilots

### 4.2 Flight conditions

AWT and AWQ are two different competitions held separately
(even during the same day). AWT pilots always fly in the
strongest air conditions. AWQ pilots fly in the softest
conditions. This is to avoid beginner competitors flying in
hard conditions and to allow fair judging for both leagues.

### 4.3 Qualifying/Downgrading

At the end of every season:

- The last 2 pilots of the overall seasonal AWT ranking are
  downgraded to AWQ
- The first 2 pilots of the overall seasonal AWQ ranking
  are qualified for the following AWT season
- If AWT isn't full due to pilots retiring, more pilots can
  climb via AWQ ranking
- No pilots outside of the top 5 of AWQ can climb to AWT

### 4.4 Competitions above ground

Only AWT pilots can register to competitions above ground
(+ AWT wildcard holders).

### 4.5 Wildcards

- **Season Wildcard:** for a pilot who couldn't attend
  previous season (injury, visa) or a valuable/talented pilot
  for the whole season
- **Event Wildcard:** same conditions but for one event only.
  If an AWT pilot cannot attend, the spot may be offered to
  AWQ top pilots
- **World Tour Champion Wildcard:** a pilot who already won
  the AWT at least once in Solo gets an automatic wildcard.
  Can request season or event wildcard

### 4.6 Missing AWT pilot

Missing one stop for unclear reasons: penalty of not being
selected to the superfinal and possible expulsion for the
following season. If missed due to reasons out of control,
may apply for a wildcard for the following season.

### 4.7 Podiums

Three podiums per stop: World Tour Podium, World Qualifier
Podium, and Women podium.

Women have an overall podium as soon as they compete in AWQ.
If a woman qualifies for AWT, she can compete in AWQ events
as well to compete in the women ranking. This ranking doesn't
affect the Qualifying/Downgrading process.
