# FAI Sporting Code Section 7B - Paragliding Aerobatics 2025 (Synchro)

This document contains all synchro-specific rules extracted from
the FAI Sporting Code Section 7B 2025.

Solo and Synchro shared manoeuvres (section 1.1 of the main
document) also apply to Synchro.

## 1. Synchro only manoeuvres

### 1.1 Wingovers Opposite - coeff: 1.60

- Series of pendulum turns with change of direction each
  time, pilots perform the turns to the opposite direction
  of each other
- Minimum: twice to one side, twice to the other side with
  high angle (minimum 135 degrees)
- Criteria: rhythm, flow, trajectory, angle
  (the higher the better)
- Free connection
- Repetition allowed (see 3.3.4 of main document)
- Bonuses: Twisted 3.5%

### 1.2 Rodeo SAT - coeff: 1.65

- One pilot is performing a SAT while the other is spiralling
  around
- Minimum: 1 revolution around the SAT at the same altitude
- Criteria: same criteria of the SAT
- Free connection
- Bonuses: Twisted 2.5%

### 1.3 Synchro Asymmetric Spiral - coeff: 1.65

- Series of pendulum turns in the same time and direction
  during a synchro spiral
- Minimum: twice to the same side
- Criteria: rhythm, flow, trajectory, angle
  (the higher the better)
- Free connection

### 1.4 Rodeo Helicopter - coeff: 1.70

- One pilot is performing a Helicopter while the other is
  spiralling around
- Minimum: 1 spiral revolution around the helicopter at
  the same altitude
- Criteria: same as for Helicopter
- Free connection
- Bonuses: Twisted 3%

### 1.5 Bitch Switch - coeff: 1.75

- Rodeo SAT + connection to Rodeo SAT with pilots replacing
  each other
- Minimum: 2 spiral revolutions around the SAT at the same
  altitude for each pilot
- Criteria: same as for SAT
- Free connection

### 1.6 Bitch Switch Helicopter - coeff: 1.75

- Rodeo Helicopter + connection to Rodeo Helicopter with
  pilots replacing each other
- Minimum: min 1 revolution of spiral
- Criteria: same as for SAT
- Free connection

### 1.7 Synchro Half Pipe Combo - coeff: 1.75

- Straight entry, minimum 45 degrees climb before rotation
  & 180 degrees spin, straight horizontal dive, exit
  180 degrees from the direction of entry, in transition use
  the exit energy of the first Half Pipe for the next one.
  Direction must be changed
- Criteria: importance of pitch on entry, 180 degrees
  rotation, maintenance of shape, strong dive, no rotation
  on entry and exit
- Twisted: twisted on the entry, untwisted by the rotation
- Full Twisted: keep the twist during the whole manoeuvre
  from beginning till exit as well as during the whole
  rotation. No change of direction of the twist. Twist shall
  be kept for at least 3 seconds or until the next trick
- Free connection
- Bonuses: Twisted 3.5%, Twisted Exit 5.5%

### 1.8 Bro Hook - coeff: 1.80

- One pilot flies straight, the other pilot approaches from
  behind and connects
- Minimum: 4 seconds connected
- Free connection
- Repetition allowed (see 3.3.4 of main document)

### 1.9 Synchro Spiral - coeff: 1.80

- Minimum: 2 revolutions and a maximum distance of one
  paraglider line length between (approx. 10 metres)
- Free connection
- Bonuses: Double or triple touch in one turn 2%

### 1.10 Wingovers Cruzados - coeff: 1.85

- Same as Wingover Opposite but the pilots must cross each
  other during the manoeuvre
- Free connection
- Repetition allowed (see 3.3.4 of main document)

### 1.11 360 Synchro Spiral to Half Pipe Combo - coeff: 1.85

- Combination of a Synchro Spiral and Half Pipe Combo
- Criteria: after a turn (360 degrees) of Synchro Spiral the
  pilots connect to a half pipe and exit it towards each other
  and start another 360 degree turn to the other direction
  in Synchro Spiral, after the turn another connection to
  a Half Pipe
- Free connection

### 1.12 Bro Hook Lateral Wings - coeff: 1.85

- One pilot flies straight twisted, the other approaches
  from behind and connects. After the connection both pilots
  must position their wings in lateral position - inside
  tip touch
- Minimum: 4 seconds
- No connection to other Bro Hook manoeuvres
- Repetition allowed (see 3.3.4 of main document)

### 1.13 Bro Hook Spiral - coeff: 1.90

- Start as a Bro Hook. After the connection the pilots must
  dive into a spiral
- Minimum: 4 seconds
- Twisted Bro Hook Spiral: one pilot has to stay twisted
  from entry until exit
- Free connection
- Repetition allowed (see 3.3.4 of main document)
- Bonuses: Twisted 2%

### 1.14 Pitch Pendulum Synchro (Molineti) - coeff: 1.95

- Minimum: twice, one over the other
- Twisted: both pilots must stay twisted during at least
  2 revolutions
- Free connection
- This manoeuvre can **only** be one of the last two
  manoeuvres
- Repetition allowed (see 3.3.4 of main document)
- Bonuses: Twisted 6%

### 1.15 Bro Hook Wingovers - coeff: 1.95

- Wingover but in Bro Hook position. Both pilots must stay
  connected during the manoeuvre until the gliders stabilise.
  Series of pendulum turns with change of direction each time
- Minimum: twice to one side, twice to the other side with
  135 degrees angle
- Criteria: rhythm, flow, trajectory, angle
  (the higher the better)
- Twisted Bro Hook Wingovers: one pilot has to stay twisted
  from entry until exit
- Free connection
- Repetition allowed (see 3.3.4 of main document)
- Bonuses: Twisted 2%

### 1.16 Gearbox Damage - coeff: 2.00

- Minimum 720 degrees synchro spiral then exit max 4 turns
  synchro SAT and then again synchro spiral at least
  360 degrees
- Criteria: closeness in the spirals (2 touches for max
  score), rapidity of connection
- Free connection

### 1.17 Fusion - coeff: 2.20

- Start from Bro Hook Lateral position. Both pilots spin the
  wings into deep stall with the leading edge facing
  each other
- Minimum: 4 seconds
- Criteria: maintenance of the shape, flow, entry, duration
  (min 4 sec), disconnection
- Free connection
- This manoeuvre cannot be one of the last two manoeuvres
- Repetition allowed (see 3.3.4 of main document)

### 1.18 Fusion Helicopter - coeff: 2.25

- Start from Fusion position into Helicopter
- Minimum: 1 rotation
- Criteria: maintenance of the shape, flow, entry, minimum
  1 rotation, disconnection
- Free connection

## 2. Synchro only landing manoeuvres

- **Synchro Ground Spiral** - minimum 720 degrees revolution
  when performed independently, or 360 degrees when following
  a Synchro Spiral (total 3 revolutions), height of wing tip
  below 3 metres at the lowest point
- **Wingovers Cruzado Inside Landing** - same criteria as
  Wingovers Cruzados. Minimum one pendulum to each side and
  360 degrees in spiral. After the last inside pendulum
  connection to a spiral until the water as a Synchro Ground
  Spiral
- **Wingovers Cruzado Outside Landing** - same criteria as
  Wingovers Cruzados. Minimum one pendulum to each side.
  After the last outside pendulum connection to a spiral till
  the water as a Ground Spiral performed individually
- **Pitch Pendulum Landing** - rapid and synchronised entry.
  Pilots should be vertically aligned for maximum score.
  Minimum twice, one over the other. The lower pilot must
  be less than 3 metres over the water in the last pitch.
  Forbidden above the ground
- **Airbound landing** - two gliders connected, the upper
  pilot has feet in the lines. They exit just before landing.
  Minimum 3 sec connected together. Forbidden above the ground

## 3. Scoring in Synchro

Same as Solo with the following changes:

- A Synchronisation mark (S) is given
- Distribution: 20% Technical, 20% Choreography, 20% Landing,
  40% Synchronisation
- The bonus does not apply to the Synchronisation mark

### 3.1 Final score formula (Synchro)

```text
Technical final score       = T * TC * 20%
Choreo final score          = C * 20%
Landing final score         = L * 20%
Synchronisation final score = S * 40%
Bonus = (Technical final + Choreo final) * B%
Final score = Technical final + Choreo final + Landing final
            + Synchronisation final + Bonus
```

### 3.2 Choreography in Synchro

Same as Solo except:

- Not compulsory to have an equal number of left and right
- Each time the pilots' wings touch each other during a run,
  each pilot earns a bonus reward (see 3.4)

### 3.3 Landing in Synchro

- Approach and precision of the raft landing (3/10)
- Ground Spiral (3/10), Mirror Ground Spiral (4/10) or
  Synchro Ground Spiral (5/10)
- Other tricks: hand and foot touch, Jesus walk, spin (2/10)

### 3.4 Synchronisation scoring

- General synchronisation appreciation (5/10)
- Synchronisation achieved during all single manoeuvres (4/10)
- Majority of synchro tricks flown compared to solo
  tricks (1/10). If equal number, half the point is given
  (0.5/10)

### 3.5 Synchro touch bonus

A bonus of 2%/touch (max 6% per run) is awarded each time
the gliders of a team touch each other.

### 3.6 Repetition in Synchro

When the two pilots perform the same manoeuvre simultaneously
in opposite directions (mirror), the manoeuvre is considered
as neutral, without a specific direction.

### 3.7 Synchro pairs

Pairs can be of the same or different nationalities. Overall
score = sum of all task scores. Highest score wins.

No team competition in Synchro.

## 4. Synchro-specific AWT/AWQ rules

- Synchro is excluded from competitions above ground
- A minimum of 2 runs is required to validate
  the championships in Synchro
